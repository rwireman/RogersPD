Mission Statement

It is the mission of the Rogers Police Department to provide the highest levels of proactive and responsive service to the City of Rogers in partnership with neighborhoods and the community. We shall endeavor to detect and solve problems that will afford the citizens of Rogers with the highest quality of life possible.
This service shall be provided with integrity, equity and excellence.

Vision Statement

The Rogers Police Department is committed to providing outstanding service with fairness, justice and quality for all in an effort to establish and maintain the highest possible quality of life for all persons in Rogers, Texas. To this end, we shall interact with the community through a community policing approach to solve the problems of crime, safety and fear of crime in our community.
